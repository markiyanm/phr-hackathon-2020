Campaign Management Portal

Phreesia's Campaign Management Portal (CMP) allows companies to create and manage Phreesia campaigns.

CPM allows you to set up a campaign to collect patient leads based on a variety of patient demographics and filters.

Once a campaign is created, Phreesia will collect patient enrollments into your program and will deliver qualified leads to your dashboard which can then be exported into your lead management system of choice.

Please note this is a hackathon demo and not a production application.
